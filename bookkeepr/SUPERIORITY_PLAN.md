# BOOKKEEPR vs QUICKBOOKS - FUNCTIONAL SUPERIORITY PLAN

## What QuickBooks Online Does Well:
1. Bank feed connections (auto-import transactions)
2. Invoice creation & payment tracking
3. Basic reporting (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
4. Tax categorization (Schedule C)
5. Payroll integration
6. Mobile app

## What QuickBooks Online SUCKS At:
1. ❌ AI categorization (manual 90% of the time)
2. ❌ Duplicate detection (creates duplicates constantly)
3. ❌ Reconciliation (painfully manual)
4. ❌ Real-time insights (reports take forever)
5. ❌ User experience (clunky, slow, confusing)
6. ❌ Customer support (terrible)
7. ❌ Pricing ($30-200/month!)
8. ❌ Learning curve (steep)
